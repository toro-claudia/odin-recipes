Tomato Egg Recipe

Description

This twist on traditional scrambled eggs not only provides a depth to your average omelet, but was a staple for me growing up and led me to providing my own twist for me to pass down. Truthfully, I never liked the taste of plain eggs and always wanted it to taste more, not just decorated with other ingredients such as cheese or ketchup (gross, sorry.) This tomato egg recipe does have a more savory approach than most recipes, I will warn you, however if this sounds like something you'd like I urge you to try it!

This recipe is for 2 people, however adjust each ingredient depending on your preference of tomatoes and level of savoryness.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 to 1 tomato
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 stalk of green onions
  • 1 tsp of chicken flavor granules
  • 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes to your desired size. Heat up a pan with oil and add in your tomatoes.
  2. In a bowl, crack your eggs and add in your fish sauce and chicken granules. Beat until well incorporated
  3. Once the tomatoes have gotten soft, add in your eggs. Stir lightly until cooked.
  4. Remove off heat and eat alone or with a side of rice!
